How We Work

A straightforward process from first conversation to final walkthrough.

01

Discovery Call

We talk through your goals, timeline, and budget, and determine if we’re the right fit for your project.

02

Site Visit & Scope

On-site review of existing conditions, followed by a clear written scope with next steps and ballpark pricing.

03

Plan, Build, Manage

We coordinate trades, manage schedules, and keep you updated while work is completed to agreed standards.

04

Walkthrough & Closeout

Final review, punch-list completion, and handoff of any relevant documentation, warranties, or permits.
